Our Vision

To build a strong and informed society where learning, creativity, and social unity grow together, making Souhrida Grandhalayam and Souhrida Barroty Club a hub of progress and togetherness.

Our Mission

To build a united and progressive community by empowering all age groups and promoting cultural, educational, and social activities. We strive for inclusiveness and harmony while working towards a drug-free society.

About Us

Founded in 2009, Souhrida Kala Kayika Samithi Barrotty serves society without political, religious, or caste boundaries. We bring together people of all ages through groups like Souhrida Balavedhi, Youvajana Sangham, and Swasraya Sangam. With Kudumbasree and NYK affiliation, we continue to play a crucial role in community growth and unity.
